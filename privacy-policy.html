Matters

Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 25, 2026

Your data belongs to you. Always. Matters is built on the belief that privacy is a fundamental right, and every decision in how this app is designed reflects that.

01

Who We Are

Matters is an iOS application created to help you capture and reflect on the moments that truly matter to you. References to "we," "us," or "our" in this policy refer to Zsolt Stelkovics, the developer of the Matters app.

02

What Information Matters Collects

Matters only processes information that you actively choose to enter. This includes:

  • Text entries describing moments or reflections you choose to record
  • Photos you choose to attach to your entries
  • Categories and labels you create to organise your moments

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other contact or identifying information.

Information We Do Not Collect

Matters is designed to collect as little data as possible. We do not collect:

  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or behavioural data
  • Crash reports or diagnostic information
  • Any information about your contacts, calendar, or other apps
  • Cookies or tracking technologies of any kind

03

How Your Data Is Stored

All data you enter in Matters is stored using Apple CloudKit — Apple's cloud storage service. This means:

  • Your data is stored in your personal iCloud account, which you control
  • Matters itself does not operate or maintain any servers that store your personal data
  • Your data is protected by Apple's security infrastructure and your Apple ID credentials
  • You can delete your data at any time within the app or through your iCloud settings

Because your data lives in your own iCloud account, it is also governed by Apple's iCloud Terms and Conditions and Privacy Policy.

04

How We Use Your Information

The content you enter in Matters is used solely to provide the app's core functionality. We do not use your content for any other purpose:

  • We do not analyse your entries for advertising or marketing purposes
  • We do not sell your data to third parties
  • We do not share your data with any third parties
  • We do not use your data to train artificial intelligence or machine learning models

05

Third-Party Services

Matters uses Apple CloudKit to store your data. Apple acts as a data processor in this context. We encourage you to review Apple's Privacy Policy to understand how Apple handles data stored in iCloud.

Other than Apple CloudKit, Matters does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

06

Data Retention and Deletion

Your data remains in your iCloud account for as long as you keep it there. You are in full control:

  • You can delete individual entries or all entries at any time within the app
  • Deleting the Matters app does not automatically delete your iCloud data — manage this through your Apple account settings

07

Children's Privacy

Matters is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided information through the app, please contact us and we will take steps to address it.

08

Your Rights

Because all your data is stored in your own iCloud account, you can exercise most rights directly:

  • Right of access — your data is accessible to you at all times within the app and through iCloud
  • Right to deletion — you can delete your data at any time within the app or through iCloud settings
  • Right to portability — your data is stored in your iCloud account, which you control

If you are an EEA resident, you also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

09

International Users

Matters is available worldwide. Because your data is stored in your personal iCloud account, the storage location is determined by Apple's infrastructure and your Apple account settings. By using Matters and iCloud, you consent to this in accordance with Apple's data transfer mechanisms.

10

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this document and, where appropriate, notify you through the app or the App Store.
